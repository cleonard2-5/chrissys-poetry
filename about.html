About the Author!

Hi there! I'm Christopher Leonard, a second year Computer Science major at Georgia Tech with Threads in People/Media, and a minor in Psychology. Coding is one of my passions, which is why I created this website myself to store my creative works. Another one of my passions (as you may've guessed) is writing poetry! I don't intend to become the best writer or even a popular poet (although it would be amazing if I could), but I still like writing about my thoughts and experiences in ways that sound prettier. That being said, I hope you enjoy reading my works! Have a good day :)
